Introduction

Welcome to the Flavour Kings logo samples!

Below are sets of different logo samples. The samples contain varients of typography, colour, and designs. Please note that the samples can be tailored and tweaked to your needs and are not necessarily the final result. For example, if you want the braai grid from set 1 sample 1 placed in set 3 sample 1, that change can be arranged.

Any changes that you request must be well defined and listed. Changes will be charged for at the agreed upon billing rate. Please feel free to contact I'MPOSSIBLE if you have any concerns or changes to implement.

LOGO SET 1

SAMPLE 01

"Flavour Kings lifestyle lounge"

Sample 1 contains a positive and negative space design of the braai, allowing for the logo to be visible on small and large formats. The logo uses a set of warm colours to match the look and feel of a braai.


As per the drawing and specifications provided, the typography contains a steak in place of the "A" in "Flavour Kings". The typography also contains a "Martini glass" in place of the "U" in "lifestyle lounge".

SAMPLE 02

"Flavour Kings lifestyle lounge"

This sample is almost the same as sample 1, the difference being the style of steak depicted on the braai. This style of steak matches the one in the typography of the logo "Flavour Kings".

SAMPLE 03

"Flavour Kings lifestyle lounge"

The sample provides a different layout of the typography, placing it below the graphic element of the logo. This layout Keeps the attention on the sizzling steak, before moving to the main headline of the typography "Flavour Kings".

SAMPLE 04

"Flavour Kings lifestyle lounge"

Similar to sample 3, sample 4 differentiates itself by having the typography above the graphic element of the logo.

LOGO SET 3

SAMPLE 01

"Chillas meat lounge"

For this sample I used two fonts that compliment each other and add a sense of class to the logo and brand.

A strong, bold font presents the "Flavour Kings" typography as Kings are seen to be strong and bold. A more relaxing and flowing font is then used for the "lifestyle lounge" typography as lounging is a calm and serene activity. Together, the typographic fonts create a conceptually strong look and feel to the logo and brand.

In this sample, I used a circular shaped braai as circles represent "wholeness, completion and community." I feel that this concept fits the brand well as people will be looking to commune together at Flavour Kings while they enjoy a drink or eat their meal.

LOGO SET 4

SAMPLE 01

"Flavour Kings lifestyle lounge"

The above sample combines the grid from a braai and uses perspective to transform it into the extending leg rest of a "lazy-boy" couch. The couch represents the "lounge" aspect to the "Flavour Kings" brand, and intends to present a welcoming and relaxing tone to the brand and logo.
